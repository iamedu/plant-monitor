# Plant Monitor Shopping List

## Required
- ESP32 DevKit, 1, about $6 to $10
- Capacitive soil moisture sensor v1.2, 1 to 3, about $3 to $5 each
- DHT22 or SHT30 temp/humidity sensor, 1, about $4 to $8
- BH1750 light sensor, 1, about $2 to $4
- Breadboard, 1, about $3 to $5
- Jumper wires pack, 1, about $3 to $5
- USB cable matching the ESP32 port, 1, about $3 to $5

## Optional
- 0.96 inch OLED display, 1, about $4 to $6
- 3.7V LiPo battery, 1, about $8 to $12
- Battery charger module, 1, about $2 to $4
- Project enclosure, 1, about $3 to $6

## Rough budget
- Minimal build: about $25 to $35
- Standard build: about $30 to $45
- Full build: about $45 to $65
